About Me

My history as a Software Engineer

My first step into the world of software development was with iOS. I was hugely motivated to develop for mobile devices due to their popularity. I knew that if I learned how to develop for mobile, I would be able to impact the lives countless people around the world. With the push of my good friend and fellow Software Engineering student Hicham Abou-Jaoude, I began to learn iOS development, and landed a job as an iOS Developer at Wattpad.

I like changing the world.

After an awesome work term as an iOS Developer at Wattpad, I realized just how much I loved working on a product that impacts millions of users worldwide. It was amazing to help improve the experience of Wattpad users, and I look forward to having similar experiences in the future.

I have a thirst for knowledge.

I'm continuously learning about how to write better software. I love learning about software; whether it be design/architecture, or how to build a fantastic user interface such that the code is modular and easy to maintain.
