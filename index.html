My Projects

Casa

Source

Finding and subletting temporary housing is a common problem for students at uWaterloo. Casa, a website and mobile application currently in development, assists students in finding temporary housing. It's being designed to provide students with options to search by critical search criteria, and see the most important details that determine whether or not a Casa is right for them.

Powered by Flask, Google App Engine, AngularJS, and iOS.

omi

Source

One of my very first ideas for a mobile application. I had a personal need for an organized, easy to use app which provides the ability to keep track of money people owe me, and people that I owe as well. That's why I came up with omi, which will be a simple, yet full featured app that provides this functionality. I will also be introducing group functionality, which will accomodates people (including myself) who share expenses with roommates. Stay tuned!

Powered by MongoDB, Express, Node.js, and iOS.
